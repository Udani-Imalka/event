About us

Wedding

A wedding is the most iconic day in anyone’s life, and that is why as a wedding & Event planner our approach to weddings is personal and highly attentive. We offer special packages with customizable wedding options for couples who choose destination weddings. We work closely with our clients to put together unforgettable moments for them and their guests. Our talented and experienced team will be there throughout to come up with new concepts to suit the customers’ needs, assist them in selecting the perfect venue, and guide our clients each step of the way on their special day.

Full Wedding Planning

This package is ideal for a couple that requires an end to end guidance. It covers all of the details that concern the function, including making budgets and assisting with booking venues and management of vendors and everything in between which involves assistance in selecting wedding attire, accessories, color scheme, overall theme and style of the wedding desired. Up to the day of the wedding, our team will be ever ready to assist and help you at all times. Our main goal is to get their ideas as much as possible, give professional advice and create that dreamy wedding for our clients.

This package is ideal for a couple that may have completed the planning process up to a certain level and requires guidance partially. This will mainly focus on providing our assistance and overseeing the entire function with the help of the couple.
